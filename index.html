PageLens AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Overview

PageLens AI (hereinafter referred to as "we," "our") is a browser extension. We deeply understand the importance of privacy protection and are committed to safeguarding user privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. We strictly adhere to the principle of data minimization, collecting only the limited data necessary to achieve functionality. All data processing activities are based on the user's explicit consent or are necessary for the performance of a contract.

Data Collection and Use

The data we collect primarily falls into five main categories, each with its specific purpose and necessity. User account information, including email address, username, and encrypted password, is actively provided by the user during registration and is necessary for creating a user account and performing authentication. Membership and usage data, such as membership level, subscription status, and AI usage count, are automatically recorded to implement membership benefit management, track usage limits, and bill subscription services.

To provide a personalized experience, we store the user's local settings and preferences, including bookmarks, search engine settings, homepage settings, and language preferences. This data is stored in the local browser and is actively set by the user. When a user utilizes the AI analysis feature, we process the webpage content and URL addresses that the user actively chooses to analyze. This data serves as the core input for the AI analysis function; we process it in real-time and do not store it long-term. Furthermore, to maintain normal service operation, we automatically record technical operational data such as IP addresses, request timestamps, and error logs, primarily used for service monitoring, security protection, and troubleshooting.

Data Retention Policy

We follow strict data retention principles, with different retention periods for different types of data. Immediately processed data, such as webpage content and AI analysis requests, is deleted immediately after processing and is not stored long-term. Short-term retained data includes user session information (automatically expires after 30 days), operational logs (automatically cleared after 90 days), and error records (retained for 180 days for service improvement). Long-term retained data is limited to user account information (retained for the duration of the account's existence), membership information (retained during the subscription period and for the subsequent period required by law), and payment records (retained by Lemon Squeezy for the period required by law).

Users have full control over their data and can delete their account at any time through the extension settings or contact customer service to request data deletion. For users who have stopped using the service for more than 180 days, we automatically trigger a data cleanup process to ensure no unnecessary user data is retained.

Protection of User Rights

We fully respect and guarantee users' data rights. You have the right to access your personal data, view data usage reports, and export your settings and bookmark data. You can update your account information, modify preferences, and adjust privacy options at any time. If you wish to delete data, you can request the deletion of your user account, the clearance of usage records, or the removal of all stored personal data.

You also have the right to withdraw consent, allowing you to stop specific data processing, cancel data collection authorization at any time, while still being able to use basic functions. These rights can be exercised directly through the extension's settings interface or by contacting our privacy team or customer support service.

Security Protection Measures

We have implemented multi-layered security measures to protect your data security. Technically, we use TLS 1.3 to encrypt all data transmissions, employ AES-256 encryption for storing sensitive information, implement strict role-based access control, and utilize JWT tokens for secure authentication.

At the organizational and management level, we provide privacy protection training for all employees and strictly enforce the principle of data minimization, collecting only necessary data. We conduct regular security audits, perform quarterly security vulnerability scans, and have established a 72-hour security incident response mechanism. In terms of infrastructure, we rely on the security capabilities of the Railway platform, have deployed DDoS protection and intrusion detection systems, and have formulated comprehensive data backup and disaster recovery plans.

Third-Party Service Data Sharing

To achieve specific functionalities, we share necessary data with a limited number of third-party service providers under strict restrictions. When you use the AI analysis feature, we share the webpage content and analysis prompts requested by the user with the OpenRouter AI service. This data is transmitted securely via API keys, used only for real-time processing, and is not stored. When you use the internet search feature, we share search query terms with the Serper.dev search service. These query terms are anonymized and not linked to user identity information. For payment processing, we share necessary payment information and user identifiers with the Lemon Squeezy payment service. All payment data processing complies with PCI DSS standards.

We adhere to strict principles of limited data sharing: sharing only the minimum amount of data necessary for functionality, strictly limiting the scope of use by third parties, signing data processing agreements with all third parties, and being transparent with users about all data sharing relationships.

Other Important Terms

Our data processing is based on three legal grounds: the user's explicit consent, the necessity for the performance of a contract, and legitimate interests. Based on consent, the user explicitly agrees to data processing and can withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal. Based on contractual necessity, we only process data necessary for performing the user service contract and providing core functionalities. Based on legitimate interests, we conduct processing for service improvement, security protection, etc., while ensuring it does not affect the user's fundamental rights.

Our services may involve data processing in countries such as the United States. All international data transfers follow adequacy decision standards, implement appropriate safeguard measures, and ensure the enforceability of user rights. Transfer security is guaranteed through Standard Contractual Clauses, Binding Corporate Rules, and approved certification mechanisms.

It is important to note that our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect children's data. If we discover data from a child, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us at any time to request the deletion of relevant data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify users 30 days in advance through the extension interface and notify registered users via email. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

If you have any privacy-related questions, please feel free to contact us via email at pagelensai@126.com. We are committed to responding within 48 hours and provide support in both Chinese and English.

Important Note: This Privacy Policy is an integral part of your use of the PageLens AI service. We are committed to continuously improving privacy protection measures and updating this policy in a timely manner to reflect the latest data processing practices.
